GreenPath

Smart Route Planning with Environmental Impact Tracking

What is GreenPath?

GreenPath is an intelligent route planning application that helps you navigate while being mindful of your environmental impact. We combine real-time route planning with carbon footprint calculations to help you make informed travel decisions.

Key Features

Smart Route Planning

Get accurate routes using real road networks powered by OpenStreetMap and OSRM routing engine.

Carbon Calculator

Calculate CO₂ emissions for your journey across 8 different vehicle types including electric, hybrid, and public transport.

AI Travel Assistant

Get destination insights, travel tips, and local information powered by advanced AI technology.

Location Services

Use your current location as starting point with one click for quick route planning.

Mobile Friendly

Fully responsive design that works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

Environmental Impact

See how many trees are needed to offset your journey's carbon emissions.

Supported Vehicle Types

Motorcycle 0.103 kg CO₂/km
Bus 0.089 kg CO₂/km
Train 0.041 kg CO₂/km
Flight 0.255 kg CO₂/km

Technology Stack

OpenStreetMap - Free, open-source map data
Leaflet.js - Interactive map library
OSRM - Open Source Routing Machine for real road routes
OpenRouter AI - Advanced AI for travel insights
Nominatim - Geocoding and reverse geocoding

How to Use

1

Enter Your Route

Type your starting point and destination, or use your current location.

2

View Route Details

See the route on the map with distance and estimated travel time.

3

Calculate Carbon Impact

Click the Carbon button, select your vehicle type, and see your environmental impact.

4

Get AI Insights

Click "Destination Info" to learn more about your destination from AI.
